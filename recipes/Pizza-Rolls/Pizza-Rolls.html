Pizza Rolls

Totino's Pizza Rolls for my hungry guys!

The perfect daytime snack to bring out the nostalgic teenager within you. "Mom! We need more pizza rolls stat. My friends and I are about to go straight bussin on these normies in the COD wait room."

Ingredients

  • Totino's Pizza Rolls
  • Ranch
  • (Optional)Frank's Red Hot Sauce
  1. Heat oven to 450 degrees
  2. Place the pizza rolls in the oven and cook for 20 minutes
  3. Remove the pizza rolls from the oven and allow them to cool for 5 minutes
  4. Place them on a plate with a saucer of ranch and the hot sauce if you opted for it
  5. Serve to your hungry guys
