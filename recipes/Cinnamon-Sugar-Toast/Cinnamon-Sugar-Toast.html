Cinnamon Sugar Toast

Add a little Cinnamon in your life, Monica

We all need something a little sweet to wake us up with our morning coffee. This recipe is super simple and just the right kind of sweet to start off your day.

Ingredients

  • Your sandwich bread of your choice
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar Cane
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Grab your bread and put it in the toaster
  2. Meanwhile, mix a couple tablespoons of cinnamon with a couple tablespoons of sugar
  3. When the bread is toast, lather it with butter
  4. Allow the butter to melt and then top it with the cinnamon sugar
  5. Enjoy!
