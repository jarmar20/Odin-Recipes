Jared's Famous Club Sandwich

Welcome to the Club

I concocted this recipe in my younger years as a starving young lad. I always had an affinity for french bread, melted cheese and delectable meats. This sandwich is certainly not your ordinary club. Upon your first bite, you will be transported to a world filled with some of the finest pleasures known to man...and yes, I'm still talking about the club. It's just that good!

Ingredients

  • French Bread Loaf
  • Deli ham
  • Sliced American Cheese
  • Block of Chedder Cheese
  • Head of Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Green and Red Bell Pepper
  • Green Goddess Dressing

Steps

  1. Cut the desired size of the french bread for sandwich and then cut in half
  2. Toast the bread lightly /(get a light browning/)
  3. Slice the tomoato, bell peppers thinly. Chop lettuce
  4. Cut 1//4 inch slices of the Chedder Cheese
  5. Layer the toasted bread with combination of half the sliced American Cheese and sliced Chedder Cheese
  6. Place the toast with the cheese into the toaster oven on broil for a couple of minutes. Allow for the cheese to melt and start to brown
  7. While the cheese and toast broil, assemble the lettuce, tomato, peppers and ham. Staggering the stacking between them.
  8. Once the toast is done, remove from the oven and spread goddess dressing on one side then place the sandwich contents into the toast
  9. Take the remaining cheese and place in the middle of the sandwich. It should be cold or room temperature.
  11. Serve. Tastes best when eaten soon after the bread is removed from oven. Cheese should pull apart seductively.
